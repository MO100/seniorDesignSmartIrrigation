Affordable & Smart Irrigation System

Practical precision irrigation for local farmers—low-cost sensors, LoRa connectivity, and a lightweight ML scheduler to optimize watering.

View Architecture See Timeline Standards Risk Plan

Project Highlights

System Architecture

ESP32 control, sensors (soil, temp, flow), LoRa links, weather API, ML scheduling, and relay-driven pump.

WBS & Timeline

Five phases from planning to documentation; Gantt overview from Feb 2025 to Jan 2026.

Modeling & Standards

Regression-based model for irrigation timing. Key standards: IEEE 1451, ISO 12100, LoRaWAN.

Risks & Feasibility

Technical/resource feasibility, safety considerations, and mitigations for sensor, comms, and power risks.

SMART Objectives

Specific

Deliver an affordable, farmer-friendly smart irrigation system tailored to Fresno-area needs.

Measurable

Reduce water use by 25%, increase yield by 10%, cut labor by 15%, and lower soil moisture variability by 20%.

Achievable

Leverage low-cost sensors, open-source tools, and concise training materials.

Relevant

Targets irrigation inefficiency to save resources, stabilize soil moisture, and support sustainability.

Time-bound

Reach goals within one growing season after deployment.
